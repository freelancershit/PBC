CURRICULAR OFFERINGS


A. PRE-ELEMENTARY EDUCATION

PBCSSI combined the salient features of traditional method of teaching with the modern, internationally proven, interactive and exploratory approaches, giving the curriculum the flexibility and effectiveness that will ensure the occurence of learning.

B. ELEMENTARY EDUCATION

Educators today are faced with the challengeof understanding and integrating technology with the traditional courses of studies to enhance learning. To meet this need, PBCSSI prepared a curriculum wherein the rather regimental courses of study are interwoven with inovative techniques like explanatory and multimedia-aided techniques and subjects like Computer, Phonics and Speech.

C. SECONDARY EDUCATION

The required curriculum with its salient features and additional electives like Computer, Statistics and Journalism has been adopted. It is student-centered and ccommunity-oriented organization content is multi-disciplinary. It is cognitive, affective, manipulative based. It focuses on critical thinking to promote creativity and productivity at all levels. It also give emphasis on technology in Science and Home Economics. It help develops work skills, acquire knowledge and form ethics and values essential for making an intelligent choice of future career. Its social studies and values development hopes to inculcate love for God and fellowmen, patriotism, nationalism and encourage involvement in public and civic affairs.
