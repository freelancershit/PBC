SERVICES


A. LIBRARY SERVICES

The school library is compiled with the minimum selections required by the DepEd for each level. It has a card catalog and reading areas to suit every students' needs. It is also made air-conditioned and comfortable to provide an environment conducive to learning. The librarian is on stand by to help any students in need. Students can go to the library anytime it is open as long as it is not in conflict with their class schedules.

B. GUIDANCE / COUNSELING

PBCSSI has a guidance counselor and a resident pastor to help students solve various problems in school or home. The office of the guidance counselor and the resident pastor is open anytime and the students are encouraged to confess their worries and problems. Both the guidance counselor and the resident pastor has experience and the capacity to help the students in different aspects.

C. SECURITY SERVICES

The PBCSSI security guards and local barangay police shall see to it that once inside the campus the students are secured and free from physical harm. They will also be available for help in any case of any untoward incident or problems.

D. CANTEEN SERVICES

The PBCSSI Canteen is operated by the school administrative staff. They serve as an arm of the school in providing the students the right kind of food for the growing children. The canteen personnel complies with the standard quality of food as prescribed by the government and food health organization. Food items are being sold at very reasonable prices. Proper eating habits and table manners in public eating places are directly and indirectly taught.

E. LABORATORY AND SPORTS SERVICES

The computer laboratory is airconditioned and offers a 2:1 student to computer ratio. The student can avail all of the laboratory facilities as may be required by the subjects under the supervision of their respective subject teachers. PBCSSI sports facilities and equipments are also at the students' use or disposal as part of their privelege and requirement so long as the student is enrolled and follows the rules of use and return.
